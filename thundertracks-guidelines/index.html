ThunderTracks
Mini Brand Guidelines

ThunderTracks is a brand built around one simple idea: sound as movement.

The visual identity combines the symbolism of an audio speaker and a road track to express power, speed, and freedom.

This mini brand guideline defines the core elements of the identity — logo system, typography, colors, usage rules, and applications across print, digital media, and merchandise — ensuring a consistent visual language across every touchpoint.
