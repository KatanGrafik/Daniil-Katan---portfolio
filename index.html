About

Daniil Katan

Brand Designer based in Český Krumlov, Czechia.

I turn brand ideas into cohesive visual systems.

My approach starts with reduction — stripping a brand's idea down to what matters, then holding that same idea consistently across logo, typography, color and real-world applications, rather than treating the logo as an isolated piece.

Depending on the project, that system can extend across packaging, social media, advertising campaigns, print materials and motion. The goal is a visual language that stays consistent while flexible enough to work across formats.

Brand identity projects

A selected set of identity projects focused on logo systems, visual language, typography, color, packaging and brand applications.

SØLVE — Skincare Branding & Packaging Design

SØLVE is a premium skincare brand built on clarity, simplicity and conscious care. The brand focuses on essential formulas and a minimal approach to both product and design.

Inspired by Scandinavian aesthetics, SØLVE avoids visual noise and unnecessary complexity. Its identity uses a muted skin-tone palette, soft typography and spacious compositions to create a calm, refined skincare experience.

Care, simplified.

BREWE — Cafe Brand Identity

BREWE is a modern urban coffee shop built around a mindful approach to coffee and time. It rejects the rush and noise of the city, treating coffee not as a quick drink on the go, but as a calm daily ritual.

The brand's central idea is the journey: coffee's path from origin to brewing, and a person's path from the external rhythm of the city to an inner state of calm.

This idea is expressed through a consistent system of icons, where every stage of coffee preparation becomes part of one visual story running through every touchpoint with the brand. The goal was to create a visual system that conveys the brand's atmosphere without saying a single word about coffee.

Slow down. Focus. Brew.

ORBIX — AI Brand Identity

ORBIX is a brand identity concept for an AI startup building tools for business process automation and computer vision. The main challenge was to make artificial intelligence feel not intimidating, but clear and approachable.

The logo combines two symbols: a loading indicator that reflects constant movement and growth, and a star at its center, standing for intelligence and the key point of the whole system. Together they form the image of a system in constant motion, rather than a static mark.

A gradient palette blending blue, pink and purple was chosen deliberately, instead of the dark neon aesthetic typical of tech brands. The goal was to convey innovation without excessive aggression — a brand that feels modern and trustworthy at the same time.

Intelligence in motion.

THUNDERTRACKS — Brand Identity for Subwoofers

THUNDERTRACKS is a brand identity for a car audio brand producing subwoofers, speakers and amplifiers made for the road.

The logo combines two symbols in one mark: a speaker diffuser shown in perspective and a road lane. A subtle tilt creates a sense of forward motion — as if the sound is moving toward you, rather than staying static.

The combination of blue and grey makes the identity feel technological, precise and engineered. This is a brand that speaks not about noise, but about thoughtful engineering.

This is your sound. At full volume.

Instagram Educational Campaign

Speak With Confidence — English Learning Campaign

Speak With Confidence is a series of Instagram banners designed to promote spoken English through confidence-based messaging rather than traditional grammar-focused advertising.

The campaign uses bold typography, emotional triggers and relatable everyday situations to show how language skills create opportunities in work, travel and daily communication.

CONFIDENCE STARTS WITH COMMUNICATION.

English Learning Instagram Campaign Social Media Design Educational Marketing

LinkedIn Thought Leadership Visuals

Future of Work — Technology & AI Content Design

Future of Work is a series of LinkedIn banners built around one editorial principle: strong ideas deserve strong visuals to match them.

Each banner pairs one provocative idea — about programming, interfaces, artificial intelligence or automation — with a visual metaphor that makes it tangible: a keyboard key as a symbol of digital literacy, a monitor hanging from a crane hook as an image of digital architecture. The composition is built around expressive typography as the main visual element, while photography serves as context rather than decoration.

The goal was to turn complex technology topics into something worth talking about — not to explain, but to make people think.

THE FUTURE BELONGS TO THOSE WHO ADAPT.

AI Content LinkedIn Design Educational Visuals Technology

Instagram Story Advertising

Consumer Campaigns — Social Media Advertising Collection

Consumer Campaigns is a series of Instagram Story ads created for consumer brands across fitness, wellness, food delivery, travel and home services.

Each banner is built around one key audience insight — a need, a doubt, or a desired outcome. The visual hierarchy flows top to bottom: the headline stops the eye, the body copy unfolds the idea, and the call to action completes the communication. Photography and typography are adapted to each category — energetic and expressive for fitness, soft and minimal for wellness, warm and friendly for food delivery.

Every project followed the same constraint: stop the user within three seconds, land the main idea, and prompt them to act.

ATTENTION FIRST. ACTION SECOND.

Printed materials & packaging

Packaging systems and printed brand materials created to turn visual identity into tangible product experiences.

SNACK PACKAGING

MunchVerse — Cosmic Snack Packaging

MunchVerse is a snack brand concept built around one idea: every flavor is its own planet. The packaging turns each cookie into an entire world — the ChocoCore flavor appears as a planet with a molten chocolate core, with an astronaut floating nearby for scale. The illustration does the storytelling: the character of each flavor comes through before the customer even reads a single word. The goal was to make the space theme feel both playful and appetizing — not science fiction, but a snack universe of its own.

The project turns ordinary snacks into a small cosmic adventure through expressive packaging, playful illustration and a bright product story.

Packaging Snack brand Illustration Print layout

FUNCTIONAL BEVERAGE PACKAGING

NURA — Functional Beverages For Modern Performance

NURA is a functional beverage brand created for people who care not only about the amount of energy, but about the quality of their state.

The system is built around three product modes — Energy, Focus and Relax — each represented by a distinct octopus mascot and a clear functional purpose.
